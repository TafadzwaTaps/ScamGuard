01

Who We Are

ScamGuard AI is a community-powered fraud and scam detection platform serving users primarily in Zimbabwe and across Africa. We help individuals verify phone numbers, URLs, and messages for fraud signals before engaging with them.

For the purposes of data protection law, ScamGuard AI is the data controller for personal data collected through this platform.

03

Why We Collect It — Legal Basis

Under GDPR and POPIA, we must have a lawful basis for processing personal data:

  • Contract performance — to provide the account and reporting service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interest — to detect fraud patterns, protect the community, and prevent abuse of the platform.
  • Legal obligation — to maintain security logs and respond to lawful requests from authorities.
  • Consent — where you explicitly submit information (e.g. a scam report), you consent to that data being stored and used for community protection.
04

How We Use Your Data

  • To run fraud and scam detection analysis on submitted entities.
  • To aggregate community reports and compute risk scores.
  • To prevent duplicate reports and spam from the same user.
  • To enforce rate limits and protect the platform from abuse.
  • To send a single confirmation email when you register (via Supabase Auth).
  • To maintain security logs for incident investigation.

We never use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

06

Cookies & Local Storage

ScamGuard does not use tracking cookies. We use your browser's localStorage solely to store your authentication token (JWT) so you remain logged in between sessions. This data:

  • Never leaves your device except when sent as a Bearer token to our own API.
  • Expires automatically when the JWT expires (typically 1 hour, refreshed on activity).
  • Is cleared immediately when you log out.
  • Is not readable by any third-party script.

No advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party tracking pixels are used on this platform.

07

Data Retention

  • Account data: retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Reports: retained indefinitely as they form the community fraud database. They are anonymised — your email is never shown publicly. You may request deletion of your specific reports.
  • Security logs (IP addresses): retained for 90 days for security purposes, then automatically deleted.
  • Anonymous scan checks: retained for up to 12 months for analytics, then deleted or aggregated.
08

Your Rights

Under GDPR and POPIA, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification — correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") — request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • Right to Restriction — ask us to stop processing your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any right, email us at privacy@scamguard.app with the subject line "Data Request". We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In Zimbabwe: the Postal and Telecommunications Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe (POTRAZ). In the EU: your national DPA.

09

Security Measures

We take security seriously and have implemented the following technical safeguards:

  • Encryption in transit: all data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS 1.2+.
  • Encryption at rest: database encrypted by Supabase (AES-256).
  • Authentication: passwords are never stored — Supabase Auth uses bcrypt hashing.
  • JWT tokens: short-lived (1 hour), signed with HS256, verified server-side on every request.
  • Rate limiting: all endpoints rate-limited to prevent brute force and DoS attacks.
  • Input sanitisation: all user input is validated, sanitised, and length-limited before processing.
  • Security headers: Content-Security-Policy, X-Frame-Options, HSTS, and other OWASP headers on every response.
  • Row-Level Security: Supabase RLS policies restrict database access per user.

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by GDPR Article 33.

Children's Privacy
